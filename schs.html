Scaredy Cat: Hide and Seek

Info

Information
You find yourself stuck inside of Kitty's mansion, you have to escape! Join your friends (or foes) in Scaredy Cat: Hide and Seek. Try and hide from the Seeker... if you can.
There are power-ups for both teams which can be used against each-other.
 
To play:
- Command blocks must be enabled.
- Cheats must be on, or one or more players have to be OP.
- Difficulty must be peaceful.
- You must have Optifine installed, with Dynamic Lights enabled.
