StringWars

Info

MULTIPLAYER: 2 players minimum, 8 maximum
 
Information:
● StringWars is a PvP game where you fight to determine what the best string instrument truly is. Kills score you points, and the team with the most points win! Each player can choose a kit to suit their playstyle.
 
●Weapons:
- Bow - Medium Damage, Long Range, low Charge Time, Medium Armour
- Trident - High Damage, Low Range, Medium Charge Time, Low Armour
- Crossbow - High Damage, Long Range, High Charge Time, High Armour
 
● Assists:
- Air Raid - Summons 1 Phantom
- Iron Wall - Summons an Iron Golem as a shield
- Proximity Slime - Summons a slime that affects nearby enemies with Wither
 
●Specials:
- Missile - Lightning strikes 3 random targets
- Detonate - Instantly damages nearby opponents
- Team Boost - Heals all teammates and prevents then taking damage for a period
